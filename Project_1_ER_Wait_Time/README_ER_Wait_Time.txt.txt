### ER Wait Time Optimization Dashboard

**Summary:**  
This project analyzes emergency room wait times across different urgency levels, days of the week, and shifts. I used Excel to clean the data and Power BI to create a dashboard highlighting operational bottlenecks and peak hours.

**Tools Used:** Excel, Power BI  
**Skills Highlighted:** Data cleaning, pivot tables, dashboard creation, time-based analysis

**Key Features:**  
- Average wait time by urgency level and day of week  
- Visuals: bar charts, line graphs, slicers  
- Staffing recommendations based on peak times
